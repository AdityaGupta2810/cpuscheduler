Why CPU Scheduling Matters

Efficient scheduling algorithms are crucial for optimal system performance and resource utilization.

Maximize CPU Utilization

Keeps the CPU as busy as possible, ensuring no processing power goes to waste.

Minimize Waiting Time

Reduces the time processes spend waiting in the ready queue.

Fair Allocation

Ensures fair distribution of CPU time among all processes.
